Description


Features of the app

It is going to be a unique app with which one can report any emergency situation/ incident in seconds with only 4 instant taps using your smartphone IOS / Android. It is going to be different than the other conventional methods of reaching out to emergency departments as It will provide evidence of the incident on the spot in the form of image, video or audio clip. It can give an clear and detailed information of the situation. In some situations, calling the cops can get the person in trouble making a video, taking picture or recording audio clip would reduce the risk of being overheard by the culprit. The location can be automatically shared which will save time and damage can be minimized. It will have super user friendly and simple interface so that the user doesn’t get stuck in complications. This app is going to be different as it can be used to report all types of incidents in one app unlike Emergency+, Fires near me etc. It will also have the option of hiding the identity of reporting person as many crimes are not reported because of the fear identity disclosure. They don’t have to go anywhere to report it. Which are some of the main reasons people don’t report crime. Help provide first hand visual or audio for best response and times. It will work in online mode (photo and videos included) or offline mode (no video or photo included only for quick sending I.e., the text only report) where there are limited signals.

Types of emergencies it will service
Police
Ambulance
Fire

Steps involved in the operation of the app:

Following are the steps involved in operation of the app
Take photo, audio or short video
Select type of incident / response from a predefined list e.g., fire in building, Graffiti reporting, robbery, car accident etc.
Auto send location or manually enter you address
Optional details of the incident


Intended Use of the APP

It will be useful to both common man and the government or the related department. It will help reduce impacts of incidents or crime. It will have a record of the incidents as well. The visual or audio clip sent by the user can be used as an evidence. It can help them solve criminal cases
