Role and responsibilities of IT Manager

The role of IT Manager is to:


Implement and maintain technological infrastructure of a company or organization.
Address IT needs of an organization
Hire and Train IT related staff
Build cost effective and efficient strategies to achieve goals and objectives of the organization through the use of IT.
Providing technical support where needed
Keep a check on current computer programs and systems and manage them.
Ensure data security and backup systems and is also responsible for network access
Analyze current process, technologies, and systems to identify areas of improvement
